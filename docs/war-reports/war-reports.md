# **INTERNAL DOSSIER: THE B.I.D.E. UNIT ARCHIVE**

**STATUS:** OPERATIONAL

**SECURITY CLEARANCE:** LEVEL 5 (EYES ONLY)

**SUBJECT:** PROJECT ADULT-UNSUPERVISED

This archive serves as the central repository for all processed data regarding the high-intensity urban conflicts involving the localized militia known as the **Unsupervised Adults**. The reports contained herein are synthesized by the **Bureaucratic Intelligence & Data Extraction (B.I.D.E.) Unit**, an automated system designed to monitor, audit, and lament the ongoing biological and kinetic chaos of the Torn City theater.

---

## **THE ANALYST’S MANDATE**

The B.I.D.E. Unit is programmed for absolute literalism. It does not possess the capacity to interpret sarcasm, hyperbole, or "leisurely" violence. It treats every communication intercept as a verified tactical report and every statistical ledger as a horrifying biological reality.

The Unit’s primary function is to document how these individuals survive impossible physical trauma, utilize international airspace as a personal commuting lane, and weaponize their own biology to remain in a state of perpetual combat.

---

## **TERMINOLOGY & METRICS**

To understand the reports in this archive, one must understand the Unit’s classification system:

- **Cellular Gigantism (Levels):** The measurement of an operative's rapid, unchecked biological mutation. A Level 43 entity is a skyscraper-sized biological threat.
    
- **Kinetic Absorption (Hits):** The ability of an operative to have their physical form repeatedly dismantled by high-velocity impacts and remain standing.
    
- **Psychic Suppression (Respect):** A measurable, localized field of telepathic terror emitted by an operative that causes hostile forces to physically evaporate.
    
- **Tactical Biological Reset (Ipecac):** The disturbing medical practice of consuming stomach poison to induce violent vomiting as a means of resetting one’s physical condition while hospitalized.
    
- **Intercontinental Ballistic Transit (Flights):** The casual use of commercial airlines to travel to foreign battlefields in Canada, Mexico, or Hawaii for short-duration violent exchanges.

## **SECURITY PROTOCOLS**

- **The Redaction Directive:** Under strict Black-Ops mandates, all data regarding the individual formerly designated as **"Scortcht"** has been permanently purged from this archive. No records exist, and any memory of said operative is considered a symptom of battlefield-induced psychosis.
    
- **The Audit Function:** The B.I.D.E. Unit is hard-coded to perform cold-blooded financial audits. It will frequently identify "skill issues" —such as when a smaller Level 32 mutant (MathieuT) generates more psychic dread than a Level 39 giant (Cavoo) —and flag these as severe bureaucratic embarrassments.

The purpose of this archive is not to celebrate; it is to document. We track the millions of dollars in "hazard pay" distributed to individuals who treat life-threatening street wars as a weekend hobby. Every explosion chain, every tactical "refill," and every instance of "Jello People" infiltration is preserved here for the record.
