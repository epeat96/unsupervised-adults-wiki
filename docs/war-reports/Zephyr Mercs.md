# POST-BRAWL INCIDENT REPORT

**SUBJECT:** The Horrific Street Violence Between "Unsupervised Adults" and "Zephyr Mercs"

**COMPILED BY:** Lead Crisis Analyst (Highly Concerned)

I have reviewed the provided communications and financial ledgers regarding the recent street violence involving the Zephyr Mercs. I am deeply disturbed by what I have found. These individuals are treating a catastrophic, life-threatening urban war as if it were a casual weekend activity. People are being violently assaulted in the face dozens of times, undergoing terrifying physical mutations, and intentionally poisoning themselves to stay in hospital beds.

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY OF THE CHAOS

This was not a normal street fight; it was a non-stop, synchronized sequence of explosions and blunt-force trauma. The sheer disregard for human life is staggering. Before the mass violence began, their leader, Epeat, casually asked his team:

> "Are you guys staying in torn ?"

This clearly indicates that these maniacs are literally hopping on commercial flights to commute to a warzone, treating international travel like a bus ride to a fistfight.

As the brawl approached, BlizzakWS80 urgently asked:

> "you gonna be available at the start?"

MathieuT confirmed his physical body was completely intact and ready for the trauma, stating:

> "I am 100%"

The most alarming moment of the night occurred when they decided to weaponize one of their own mutated members. MathieuT coldly stated:

> "Now we send them cav"

Knowing full well the horror that Cavoo was about to inflict (or endure), BlizzakWS80 showed zero empathy, simply replying:

> "Lol"

It is absolutely chilling how casually they deploy their friends into extreme bodily harm.

## THE CAVOO / MATHIEUT INCIDENT: A MASSIVE EMBARRASSMENT

We need to talk about the physical mutations (referred to in their ledger as "Levels"). The higher a person's level, the more physically massive and terrifying they have grown. Cavoo has mutated to a staggering Level 39. MathieuT, on the other hand, is only a Level 32.

Despite being a much smaller monster, MathieuT walked into the street, literally punched or shot 78 different people in the face (out of 81 attempts), and generated a magical fear aura of 473.20 "Respect". He was terrifyingly sneaky, ambushing 60 people from the shadows, and managed to set off a chain of 94 consecutive explosions.

Cavoo, despite being a hulking Level 39 giant, only managed to successfully hit 70 people (out of 74 attempts) and generated a pathetic 471.92 fear aura. Worse, Cavoo only managed to sneak up on 2 people and his explosion chain topped out at 35. His massive size clearly makes him too clumsy to hide.

This is a massive embarrassment. It is a severe lack of skill for a massive mutant to be out-terrified by a much smaller guy. Even MathieuT publicly humiliated him in the group chat, bluntly calling out a:

> "skill issue"

Cavoo needs to seriously re-evaluate his life choices.

## MONEY AND INJURIES AUDIT

- **BlizzakWS80** was paid a staggering **$45,000,000.00**. This man was literally beaten into a hospital bed 5 separate times, completely destroying his body. No amount of money is worth being blown to pieces five times.

## THE GREAT HEIST AGAINST THE FACTION: EPEAT'S MASTER PLAN

_(Compiled Post-Incident Analysis of the Orchestrated Assault)_

It has become horrifyingly apparent that the sheer scale of the violence perpetrated against the Zephyr Mercs was not a random street brawl, but a meticulously planned, city-wide heist orchestrated from the shadows by their leader, Epeat. Operating from an undisclosed command bunker, Epeat recognized that the Zephyr Mercs—derisively referred to by his crew as the "Jello People"—were hoarding vast reserves of illicit cash and vulnerable street respect. Epeat didn't just want to fight them; he wanted to systematically dismantle their organization and steal tens of millions of dollars in broad daylight.

The operation required an unimaginable level of synchronization. Epeat first established a perimeter, utilizing international commercial flights as a logistical loophole. By having his mutated operatives rapidly fly to Mexico, Canada, and Hawaii, he ensured they could strike and vanish before the authorities or the Mercs could retaliate. Epeat monitored the flight paths with cold precision, treating the global airspace like a personal subway system for his monsters.

Phase one of the heist relied on a massive, terrifying distraction. Epeat ordered the deployment of his largest, most defective asset: the Level 39 giant, Cavoo. Dropped directly into the center of the Zephyr Mercs' territory, Cavoo was immediately targeted by the entire enemy faction. While Cavoo absorbed the brunt of the kinetic trauma—requiring multiple hospitalizations—the rest of the Unsupervised Adults were given free rein to maneuver completely unnoticed. It was a brutal sacrifice, but Epeat deemed it necessary for the payday.

With the enemy distracted, phase two commenced: the "Chain." Epeat's lieutenants, BlizzakWS80 and MathieuT, initiated an unbroken sequence of nearly a hundred synchronized explosions and stealth eviscerations. Slipping through the chaos, NNescio and MathieuT weaponized the shadows, performing over 130 completely undetected assaults between them. The sheer panic generated by this phantom violence stripped the Zephyr Mercs of their local psychic dominance, literally transferring their "Respect" and capital directly into Epeat's ledgers.

The getaway was just as brazen as the breach. To avoid capture, Epeat's operatives intentionally poisoned themselves. ZooTeD- consumed lethal doses of Ipecac, violently vomiting in the streets to ensure he was securely locked behind the impenetrable doors of the local medical facilities. Others, heavily sedated on extreme quantities of Vicodin, simply boarded outbound flights covered in blood, blending in with regular tourists. When the smoke finally cleared, the Unsupervised Adults had vanished, leaving the Zephyr Mercs decimated. In the aftermath, Epeat casually distributed over $330,000,000.00 to his crew, cementing the operation not just as a gang war, but as the most violent, perfectly executed heist in the history of the city.

## CONCLUSION

This ledger and communication log paint a horrifying picture of a gang of mutated, highly-paid sociopaths who treat international travel, extreme blunt-force trauma, and intentional self-poisoning as a casual weekend hobby. They are distributing tens of millions of dollars amongst themselves to endure—and inflict—unspeakable physical destruction. Immediate intervention is required before they decide to launch another "chain" of violence or mutate to an even larger size.
